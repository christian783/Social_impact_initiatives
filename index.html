Wildlife Photography Internship

This internship provides a unique personal and professional development opportunity in the Greater Kruger Area.Interns support wildlife conservation initiatives through the medium of photography.The program helps to increase awareness of conservation and other local and global issues.

Lion and Wildlife Sanctuary

These sanctuaries play a significant role in wildlife conservation efforts, helping to protect and preserve one of the world's last remaining wildlife strongholds.They contribute to wildlife conservation efforts and help protect endangered species and their habitats.

Wildlife Internships

These opportunities provide a stepping stone into careers in wildlife conservation.Interns engage in diverse tasks such as monitoring, rehabilitating, and managing reserves, participating in wildlife research and monitoring, maintenance projects, and caring for captive animals.

Wildlife Conservation

Impact assessment and evaluation are essential tools in addressing the financial complexities associated with wildlife conservation.Through systematic analysis of investment strategies, organizations can make informed decisions about resource allocation to ensure maximum positive outcomes for both endangered species and their ecosystems
